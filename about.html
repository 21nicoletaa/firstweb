ABOUT 1121

1121 is more than just a clothing brand — it is a statement of identity, confidence, and presence. Built from the streets and inspired by real culture, 1121 represents those who refuse to blend in. Every piece we create is designed to reflect individuality and strength.

Our vision is rooted in modern streetwear aesthetics: minimal design, oversized silhouettes, and bold attitude. We believe that clothing should not only look good, but also make you feel powerful. When you wear 1121, you’re not just wearing fabric — you’re wearing confidence.

We focus on quality, comfort, and detail. From heavyweight cotton to carefully chosen cuts, each item is crafted to deliver both style and durability. Our collections are influenced by urban environments, music culture, and the raw energy of the streets.

1121 is for the ones who move differently. For those who create trends instead of following them. For those who understand that style is not about fitting in — it’s about standing out.

This is not fast fashion. This is not basic clothing. This is 1121.
